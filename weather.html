Weather

Winter

  • Temperature

  • Winter has an average low of 40°F and an average high of 50°F.

  • Rainfall

  • From December to February it rains for an average of 8.8 days with a total of around 4.9" of rain.

  • Snowfall

  • It snows for about one 2.5 day with a total of around 2" of snow.

  • Humidity

  • The average humidity is 70.7%

Spring

  • Temperature

  • Spring has an average low of 65°F and an average high of 72°F.

  • Rainfall

  • From March to May it rains for about 10.5 days with a total of around 4.33" of rain.

  • Snowfall

  • It does not snow from May to November

  • Humidity

  • The average humidity is 67%

Summer

  • Temperature

  • Summer has an average low of 72°F and an average high of 88°F.

  • Rainfall

  • From June to August it rains for about 10.5 days with a total of around 4.1" of rain.

  • Snowfall

  • It does not snow from May to November

  • Humidity

  • The average humidity is 68%

Fall

  • Temperature

  • Autumn has an average low of 50°F and an average high of 72°F.

  • Rainfall

  • From September to November it rains for about 8.5 days with a total of around 4.1" of rain.

  • Snowfall

  • It does not snow from May to November

  • Humidity

  • The average humidity is 75%
