Where to stay

Cottages In The Clouds

These cabins sit on top of Chattanooga's Lookout Mountain and feature hot tubs, gas fireplaces, and even WiFi.

Make a reservation

Treetop Hideaway

Technically this isn't a cabin, but whose asking? Stay in a climate controlled tree house stocked with a full kitchen and bathroom. Enjoy the all of the scenic views that Lookout Mountain has to offer right from your balcony.

Make a reservation

Campground

Chattanooga campgrounds serve as your basecamp for adventures. Enjoy hiking, biking, kayaking, rock climbing, white water rafting and much more all from Chattanooga's campgrounds.

Make a reservation

The Choo Choo Hotel

The Choo Choo Hotel is an old Terminal Station that opened in 1909. You can stay in an old Pullman Train Car which has been renovated into a modern hotel room.

Make a reservation

Welcome Valley Village

Welcome Valley Village offers 5 log cabins at the base of the Ocoee River. It is nestled in the SMokey Mountains and only a few minutes from Cherokee National Forest. You can enjoy whitewater rafting, tubing, horseback riding, fishing, hiking, and mountain biking.
