Restaurants

Frothy Monkey

Frothy Monkey was one of the first coffeehouses is Nashville before expanding into Chattanooga. They serve breakfast and brunch until 5 before converting into dinning restaurant with table service for the evening. The Chattanooga location is built inside of the first Chattanooga train stations.

Make a reservation

Urban Stack

Urban Stack is a modern woodsy tavern that serves some of Chattanooga's best burgers and bourbon. It is built in a old Railway Baggage Building that is over 150 years old. Their building is LEED Certified and they work with local farmers and vendors to provide the best quality meats.

Make a reservation

The FEED Table & Tavern

The FEED restaurant is a combination of old tavern and family dinning. They serve seasonal farm fresh meals with ingredients sourced from local farms. This restaurant sits inside of an old barn that was once Chattanooga's Seed-Feed Supply Company.

Make a reservation

1885 Grill

1885 Grill serves fresh seafood, quality steaks, and southern sides. Located at the base of Chattanooga's Lookout Mountain, it is the perfect stop for when your done hiking the trails.

Make a reservation

Market South

Market South will remind you of a food hall with the multiple restaurants under the same roof. You can sit down at 5 Wits brewery for a local beer, and order from their menu which shows everything available from the other restaurants in the building.

Make a reservation
